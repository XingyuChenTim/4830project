Orange-chicken

Orange Sauce
• 1/2 cup orange juice, (120ml)
• 1 teaspoon orange zest, (1g) optional
• 1/3 cup unsalted chicken broth, (79ml)
• 2 tablespoons soy sauce , (30ml)
• 3 tablespoons honey, (45ml)
• 2 teaspoons rice vinegar, (10ml) or lemon juice
• 1/8 teaspoon red chili flakes, (add more if you like it spicy)
• 2 teaspoons cornstarch, (6g)
• 4 teaspoons water, (20ml)
Chicken
• 1 1/2 pounds boneless skinless chicken breast, (681g) cut into 1-inch cubes
• 1 teaspoon kosher salt, (2g)
• 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, freshly ground
• 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil, (2.5ml)
• 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, (30ml)
• 1 tablespoons minced garlic, (10g)
• 1 tablespoons minced ginger, (8g)
• 1 tablespoon sliced green onions, (1g)
• 1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds
Calories: 420
